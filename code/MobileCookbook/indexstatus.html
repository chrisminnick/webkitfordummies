Lemonade

  1. Mix and heat 1 cup of water and 1 cup of sugar until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add 1 cup of lemon juice.
  3. Add 2-3 cups of water, and lots of ice.
  4. Enjoy!

Soup

  1. Chop and sauté 1 onion, 1 stalk celery, and 1 carrot for a few minutes.
  2. Add 5 cups vegetable stock and 1 can diced tomatoes.
  3. Wash and then stir in 1 cup lentils.
  4. Add 1 bay leaf, salt and pepper.
  5. Add chopped serrano peppers to taste.
  6. Cook until lentils are soft.
  7. Enjoy!

Tortillas

  1. Stir together 2 cups of instant corn masa mix and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  2. Add 1 1/4 cups water and mix together well.
  3. Form balls of dough from the mix.
  4. Press balls of dough into tortillas with a tortilla press.
  5. Cook tortillas on a comal.
  6. Enjoy!
